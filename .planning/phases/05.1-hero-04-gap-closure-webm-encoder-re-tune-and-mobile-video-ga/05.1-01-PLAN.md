<objective>
Strip the structurally broken WebM ladder from the hero asset chain and tighten the `<source media>` algebra so phones (<768px) match zero video sources. This is the byte-reducing half of HERO-04 gap closure (Defect A + Defect B fix from CONTEXT.md). After this plan, a 412×823 viewport hitting `/` cannot start any video network request, and the 3 oversized WebM binaries are gone.

Purpose: VP9 with per-frame keyframes cannot hit bitrate targets — the 360p WebM ships at 4.46× its MP4 counterpart (8.47 MB vs 1.90 MB). Dropping WebM entirely is the smallest blast radius that meets the LCP gate (per CONTEXT.md D-01). Tightening the 360p and 540p media queries to start at `(min-width: 768px)` makes the mobile-video-free invariant structural rather than React-conditional (per CONTEXT.md D-04). The `!isMobile` React gate stays in place as belt-and-suspenders (D-05).

Output:
- `scripts/build-hero-ladder.sh` with the libvpx-vp9 block stripped
- `scripts/verify-hero-keyframes.sh` with the 3 WebM entries removed
- `src/content/homepage-hero.ts` with a 3-entry MP4-only sources array using bounded media queries
- 3 WebM binaries deleted from `public/hero/`
- `tests/hero/source-ladder.spec.ts` updated to assert the new 3-source ladder
</objective>
