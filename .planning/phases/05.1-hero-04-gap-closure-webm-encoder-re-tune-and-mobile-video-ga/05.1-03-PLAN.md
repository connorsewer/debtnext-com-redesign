## Why the first attempt failed

The 16,219 ms representative-run LCP on 2026-05-21 was caused by Lighthouse downloading `homepage-hero-360p.webm` (8.88 MB) at the 412x823 mobile viewport. Two structural defects compounded:
1. The `<source media="(max-width: 1023px)">` rule on the 360p WebM matched a 412 px phone, so the browser picked that source as soon as it parsed the SSR HTML.
2. The VP9 ladder was 1.85x to 4.46x its MP4 counterpart because per-frame keyframes (D-05 from Phase 5, required for GSAP scrub fidelity) are structurally incompatible with VP9 bitrate targeting.

Phase 5.1 fixes both by dropping WebM entirely and bounding the media queries at `(min-width: 768px)`.
