## Findings considered and rejected (so they aren't re-audited)

- **Product-visual fixed-px type/radius** (`text-[10.5px]`, `rounded-[5px]`, etc.):
  intentional self-contained "software chrome" per `DESIGN.md` §4.1/§7.4 — not a
  finding. Plan 004 *documents* the related `font-[500]` convention rather than
  changing it.
- **8px content-card radius vs §8.4's literal spec**: deviates from the literal
  spec but is internally consistent across the card system — settled convention,
  not worth churning.
- **"One voltage per band"**: audited clean — no band has two competing filled
  primary CTAs.
- **Marquee reduced-motion**: `StatMarquee`/`LogoMarquee` already honor
  `prefers-reduced-motion` and pause on hover — correct.
- **Two number-heavy bands back-to-back on the homepage** (ProofBand + StatMarquee):
  flagged as a hierarchy smell but the in-code comment shows it's deliberate
  ("inundate them with facts") — LOW confidence, left to the maintainer, no plan.
